# Upscale company slide — font colors (CCC reference)

Use these on the template. **Do not use pure black** on the cover headline or navy panel.

| Role | Hex | Where |
|------|-----|--------|
| **Headline gold** | `#FEDB8D` | Cover `[Presentation title]` (left, large) |
| **Logo / navy callout** | `#FFE19E` | UP upscale ai, `[Subtitle line 1]` (navy box) |
| **Meta line** | `#AABBCC` | `[Subtitle line 2 — owner / date]` |
| **Tag / workstream** | `#778899` | `[Tag / workstream]` |
| **Content title** | `#051830` | `[Slide title]` (navy, not black) |
| **Lead line** | `#556677` | `[Subtitle or lead line — optional]` |
| **Body text** | `#333333` | Bullets (softer than #000) |
| **Footer ©** | `#6688AA` / `#888888` | Cover / content footer line |

**Bad (fixed):** `#000000` on cover title or navy panel.
